How often should I have my dryer vent professionally cleaned?

For optimal safety and efficiency, we recommend professional dryer vent cleaning every 1-3 years. According to safety data found on Wikipedia, regular maintenance is the best defense against lint-related house fires. If you do many loads of laundry each week or have a long vent system, annual cleaning is necessary to prevent fire hazards in Reno-Sparks homes.

Can lint get inside the dryer itself?

Yes. When the exterior vent line is backed up, lint finds the easiest path out through gaps in the dryer base, creating a dangerous "bed of lint" inside the cabinet. This is why professional internal cleaning is a critical part of our safety audit.

Do fabric sheets cause airflow problems?

Surprisingly, yes. Fabric sheets leave a waxy residue on your lint screen over time. We recommend scrubbing your lint trap with hot soapy water and a brush every few months to ensure maximum velocity.
